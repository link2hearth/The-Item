The Item — Progression-Based Upgrade System for Minecraft Bedrock

One item. Twenty-three upgrades. Unlock everything by crafting.

The Item is a Bedrock addon built around a single concept: you receive a unique Item automatically when you join the world, then progressively unlock powerful features by crafting and applying upgrades. No commands, no permissions, no experimental APIs — just pure survival gameplay.


═══════════════════════════════════════
  HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════

When you join a world with The Item installed, you automatically receive your personal Item. Use it to access every feature through an intuitive in-game UI. Each feature is locked by default — to unlock one, craft its specific upgrade item and apply it through The Item. Once unlocked, the feature is permanently tied to your Item and can be toggled on or off in Settings.

Your Item is personal: it's bound to your name and only you can use it. If you die without the "Keep Item on Death" upgrade, you lose all your progress and start fresh with a new Item.


═══════════════════════════════════════
  UPGRADES (23 total)
═══════════════════════════════════════

▸ Quality of Life
─────────────────
• WAILA (What Am I Looking At) — Displays the name of the block or entity you're looking at on the action bar. Supports full Bedrock block name translation, including all slabs, corals, wood types, flowers, and more.
• Auto Refill — When a stack in your hand runs out, it automatically replaces it with the same item from your inventory. Never interrupt your building flow.
• Auto Replant — Automatically replants crops when you harvest them. Works with wheat, carrots, potatoes, beetroot, and nether wart.
• Dynamic Lights — Hold a light-emitting item (torch, glowstone, lantern, etc.) and the area around you lights up dynamically. Multiple light levels depending on the item.
• Item Magnet — Pulls nearby items toward you in an 8-block radius. Toggle it off when you need precision.

▸ Movement & Survival
──────────────────────
• Night Vision — Permanent night vision effect, toggleable at will.
• No Fall Damage — Completely negates fall damage.
• Agile — Grants Speed I and Jump Boost II for faster traversal.

▸ Mining & Gathering
────────────────────
• Vein Miner — Break one ore block and the entire connected vein breaks with it. Works with all ore types.
• Tree Capitator — Chop one log and the entire tree falls. Detects connected logs and breaks them all at once.
• Spawner Silk Touch — Mine a mob spawner with a Silk Touch pickaxe and receive the spawner block plus its corresponding spawn egg. Choose the mob type when placing it back.

▸ Teleportation (6 tiers)
─────────────────────────
Teleportation is unlocked progressively across 6 tiers, from basic to advanced:

  T1 — World Spawn: Teleport to the world spawn point.
  T2 — Player Spawn: Teleport to your personal bed/respawn point.
  T3 — Home: Set and teleport to a personal home location.
  T4 — Hub: Set and teleport to a shared hub (server-wide waypoint).
  T5 — Warps: Create, manage, and teleport to named warp points.
  T6 — Player TP: Teleport directly to other online players (with consent toggle).

Every teleportation saves your previous location so you can use "Back" to return.

▸ Backpack (4 tiers)
────────────────────
Extra inventory storage through an invisible companion entity:

  Cheat Backpack — Unlocks 27 bonus inventory slots (small chest).
  Tier 2 — Expands to 36 slots.
  Tier 3 — Expands to 45 slots.
  Tier 4 — Expands to 54 slots (double chest).

Access your backpack anytime through The Item. Items persist across sessions.

▸ Death & Persistence
─────────────────────
• Corpse on Death — When you die, a corpse entity spawns at your death location holding all your items. Return to your corpse and interact with it to recover your inventory. Your death coordinates and dimension are also displayed in chat.
• Keep Item on Death — Normally, dying without this upgrade resets all your progress. With it, your Item and all unlocked upgrades are preserved on death.


═══════════════════════════════════════
  CHUNK CLAIMS
═══════════════════════════════════════

Protect your builds with a chunk-based land claiming system — no upgrades required, available to all players from the start.

▸ Claiming
• Claim the chunk you're standing in through the Chunks menu.
• Each claim costs XP levels. The cost scales: the more chunks you own, the higher the price.
• The number of chunks you can claim is gated by your playtime — you need to actually play before claiming more land.

▸ Protection
• Claimed chunks prevent other players from breaking blocks, placing blocks, or interacting with containers.
• Protection works across all dimensions (Overworld, Nether, End).

▸ Trust System
• Trust specific players to allow them to build and interact within your claimed chunks.
• Untrust them at any time.

▸ Visualization
• Toggle chunk border visualization to see colored particle walls around chunks:
  – Green = your chunks
  – Blue = chunks where you're trusted
  – Red = chunks owned by someone else
  – White = unclaimed chunks

▸ Management
• View all your claimed chunks in a list.
• Teleport directly to any of your claimed chunks.
• Unclaim chunks to free up slots and recover capacity.


═══════════════════════════════════════
  ADMIN ITEM
═══════════════════════════════════════

Server operators get access to a separate Admin Item with server management tools:

• Player Management — Kick or ban players from the server. View and manage the ban list.
• Chunks Administration — View all claimed chunks across the server, claim chunks for the server (protected zones), unclaim any chunk, transfer chunk ownership between players, and set the maximum number of chunks per player.
• Server Settings — Configure server-wide parameters.
• Access to the regular player Item from the admin interface.


═══════════════════════════════════════
  SETTINGS
═══════════════════════════════════════

Every toggleable feature has its own ON/OFF switch in the Settings menu. Locked features appear grayed out until you unlock them. This lets you customize your experience:

• Toggle each passive effect individually (Night Vision, No Fall, Agile, Magnet, etc.)
• Enable/disable mining features (Vein Miner, Tree Capitator, Spawner Silk Touch)
• Turn Auto Refill and Auto Replant on or off
• Control your Cheat Backpack and WAILA
• Allow or deny incoming teleport requests from other players


═══════════════════════════════════════
  FEATURES
═══════════════════════════════════════

• No experimental APIs — Works on any Bedrock world without toggling experiments.
• Bilingual — Full English and French localization for all menus and messages.
• Per-player progression — Each player unlocks features independently.
• Server-friendly — All data persists via dynamic properties. No external files, no databases.
• Lightweight — Under 400 KB total. Pure JavaScript, no build step required.
• Compatible with Minecraft Bedrock 1.21.120+


═══════════════════════════════════════
  INSTALLATION
═══════════════════════════════════════

1. Download the .mcaddon file.
2. Double-click to import into Minecraft.
3. Activate both the Behavior Pack and Resource Pack on your world.
4. Your Item is given to you automatically — start unlocking upgrades!
