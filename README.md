# The Item

A progression-based upgrade system for Minecraft Bedrock. One item. Unlock everything by crafting.

---

## How it works

When you join a world with The Item installed, you automatically receive your personal **Item**. Use it to access all features through an in-game UI.

Every feature is **locked by default**. To unlock one, craft its upgrade item and apply it through The Item. Once unlocked, the feature is permanently tied to your Item and can be toggled on or off in Settings.

Your Item is **personal** — it's bound to your name and only you can use it. If you die without the *Keep Item on Death* upgrade, you lose all progress and start fresh.

## Upgrades

### Quality of Life
| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| WAILA | Shows the name of the block or entity you're looking at on the action bar |
| Auto Refill | Automatically replaces an empty stack in your hand with the same item |
| Auto Replant | Replants crops automatically when you harvest them |
| Dynamic Lights | Holding a light source illuminates the area around you |
| Item Magnet | Pulls nearby dropped items toward you in an 8-block radius |

### Movement & Survival
| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| Night Vision | Permanent night vision, toggleable |
| No Fall Damage | Completely negates fall damage |
| Agile | Grants Speed I and Jump Boost II |
| Double Jump | Lets you jump a second time in the air |
| Anti Void | Teleports you to safety if you fall into the void |
| Water Breathing | Permanent water breathing |

### Combat & Stats
| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| Haste | Permanent Haste effect |
| Fire Resistance | Permanent Fire Resistance |
| Regeneration | Permanent Regeneration |
| Resistance | Permanent Resistance |
| Strength | Permanent Strength |
| Saturation | Permanent Saturation |
| Double XP | Doubles experience gained from all sources |

### Weapon Enchants (Combat Builds)
Sharpness · Smite · Knockback · Fire Aspect · Life Steal · Sweeping Edge

### Mining & Gathering
| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| Vein Miner | Break one ore and the entire connected vein breaks with it |
| Tree Capitator | Chop one log and the whole tree falls |
| Spawner Silk Touch | Mine spawners with a Silk Touch pickaxe and retrieve them |

### Teleportation

Some destinations are always available, others require an upgrade.

| Destination | Requirement |
|---|---|
| World Spawn | Always available |
| Player Spawn | Upgrade: TP Player Spawn |
| Home | Upgrade: TP Home |
| Last Death | Upgrade: TP Last Death |
| Hub | Always available (if set by an admin) |
| Warps | Always available (if enabled by an admin) |
| TP to Player | Always available |

All teleportations save your previous location so you can use **Back** to return.

### Backpack (up to 5 tiers)
Sneak and use The Item to open your backpack. Items persist across sessions.

| Tier | Slots |
|---|---|
| Cheat Backpack | 27 |
| Tier 2 | 40 |
| Tier 3 | 60 |
| Tier 4 | 80 |
| Tier 5 | 96 |

Also available: **Backpack Hover** — when your inventory is full, the backpack silently absorbs nearby dropped items.

### Death & Persistence
| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| Corpse on Death | A corpse spawns at your death location holding all your items. Interact with it to recover them. |
| Keep Item on Death | Preserves your Item and all unlocked upgrades on death |

## Chunk Claims

Available to all players from the start — no upgrade required.

- **Claim** the chunk you're standing in through the Chunks menu
- Claiming costs XP levels, and scales with how many chunks you already own
- The number of claimable chunks grows with your playtime
- **Protection** prevents other players from breaking blocks, placing blocks, or using containers in your chunks — across all dimensions
- **Trust** specific players to grant them access to your claimed chunks
- **Visualize** chunk borders with colored particle walls (green = yours, blue = trusted, red = others, white = unclaimed)
- **Teleport** directly to any of your claimed chunks from the management menu

---

## Admin Item

Server operators automatically receive a separate **Admin Item** with server management tools:

- **Player Management** — Kick or ban players, view and manage the ban list
- **Chunk Administration** — View all claimed chunks server-wide, claim server-protected zones, transfer ownership between players, set per-player chunk limits
- **Server Settings** — Configure server-wide parameters
- Access to the regular player Item from the admin interface

---

## Settings

Every feature has its own toggle in the Settings menu. Locked features appear grayed out until unlocked.

---

## Installation

1. Download the `.mcaddon` file
2. Double-click to import it into Minecraft
3. Activate both the **Behavior Pack** and **Resource Pack** on your world
4. Your Item is given to you automatically — start unlocking upgrades

**Requires Minecraft Bedrock 1.21.120+** — No experimental APIs needed.
